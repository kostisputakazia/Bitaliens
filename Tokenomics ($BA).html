TOKENOMICS OVERVIEW




At Bitaliens, our tokenomics is designed to foster a vibrant and sustainable ecosystem for our community while adding tangible value to our $BA token. We’ve carefully structured the token distribution and utility mechanisms to ensure a fair and rewarding experience for all participants.
Token Distribution:

• Presale Allocation (30%): The Presale allocation will fund the project’s development, enabling us to enhance the Bitaliens experience and ecosystem.
• Airdrop Reward (5%): We’ll distribute a percentage of $BA tokens to our existing NFT holders as a gesture of appreciation for their continuous support.
• Upgrade Art Mechanism (10%): This segment will facilitate the burning of $BA tokens to upgrade art within the Bitaliens ecosystem, bringing added value to the tokens and NFTs.
• Liquidity Pool (20%): Ensuring ample liquidity for smooth trading experiences and market stability.
• Reserve (10%): Reserved for future development, partnerships, and unexpected opportunities, ensuring the long-term sustainability of the project.
• Team & Advisors (15%): Allocated with a vesting schedule, incentivizing alignment with the project’s success and long-term vision.
• Ecosystem Growth (10%): Dedicated to community incentives, strategic partnerships, and marketing efforts to foster growth and engagement within our community.
Token Utility:
$BA serves as the fundamental utility token within the Bitaliens ecosystem. Beyond its role in the initial presale, $BA tokens empower holders with opportunities to upgrade and enhance their NFT art through a burning mechanism, bringing additional value to both the tokens and the NFTs.
This transparent and community-centric approach to tokenomics aims to create an ecosystem that not only values its participants but also continually evolves through community feedback and participation.
